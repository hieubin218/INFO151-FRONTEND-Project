~ HISTORY OF CGV CINEMA COMPANY ~

CGV is the largest multiplex cinema chain in South Korea and has branches in Vietnam, China, Indonesia, Turkey, Myanmar, and the United States. CGV has original name is Theater Business Team in 1995, and established by CJ Cheil Jedang of South Korea.

CGV CINEMA OPERATION IN VIETNAM

CGV officially joined Vietnam in 2014 after CJ company collaborate MegaStar and has multiple new attractive offers. CGV cinema locate at popular cities in Vietnam are Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang,... These cinema have attracted a large amount of teenagers as well as adults to watch and enjoy the films with high quality, along with special servies and offers for custumers.
